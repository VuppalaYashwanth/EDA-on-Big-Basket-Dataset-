## 1. CATEGORY-WISE PERFORMANCE ANALYSIS

### Revenue Leaders

Our analysis reveals **Cleaning & Household** and **Beauty & Hygiene** as the top-performing categories, collectively generating over 40% of total revenue.

**Top 3 Categories by Revenue:**

1. **Cleaning & Household** - ₹3.42M (23.6%)
   - 5,141 orders | 11,363 units sold
   - Average discount: 11.68%
   - **Insight:** High-margin category with strong repeat purchase behavior

2. **Beauty & Hygiene** - ₹3.08M (21.3%)
   - 5,729 orders | 12,527 units sold
   - Average discount: 11.09%
   - **Insight:** Premium pricing maintained despite competitive market

3. **Bakery, Cakes & Dairy** - ₹2.03M (14.0%)
   - 5,361 orders | 12,680 units sold
   - Average discount: 14.11%
   - **Insight:** Higher discount rate driving volume sales

### Strategic Implications

- **Focus Area:** Cleaning & Household and Beauty & Hygiene categories show excellent margin potential
- **Growth Opportunity:** Fruits & Vegetables has the highest order volume (6,021 orders) but lower revenue per order, indicating potential for premium product expansion
- **Pricing Strategy:** Maintain competitive pricing in high-frequency categories while preserving margins in premium segments

## 3. DISCOUNT STRATEGY EFFECTIVENESS

### Impact Analysis

Our analysis reveals a **direct correlation between discount depth and revenue per transaction**, with higher discounts driving significantly increased order values.

| Discount Bracket | Transactions | Units Sold | Revenue (₹) | Revenue/Transaction |
|------------------|-------------|------------|-------------|---------------------|
| No Discount | 12,096 | 20,833 | 3,918,427 | ₹323.95 |
| 1-10% | 15,265 | 32,091 | 4,976,956 | ₹326.05 |
| 11-20% | 10,113 | 23,651 | 3,675,460 | ₹363.47 |
| **20%+** | **4,274** | **15,615** | **1,914,583** | **₹447.82** |

### Key Findings

1. **Volume Driver:** 20%+ discounts generate 38% higher revenue per transaction compared to no discount
2. **Sweet Spot:** 11-20% discount range shows optimal balance between volume and margin
3. **Customer Behavior:** Customers purchasing discounted products buy 2.2x more units per transaction

### Strategic Recommendations

- **Selective Discounting:** Reserve 20%+ discounts for strategic categories (overstocked items, seasonal clearance)
- **Tiered Approach:** Implement 10-15% discounts for volume-driving campaigns while maintaining margins
- **Personalization:** Use customer segmentation to offer targeted discounts based on purchase history
- **Bundle Strategy:** Create product bundles at 11-20% discount to increase basket size

- **Premium Push:** Introduce more ₹300-500 products in high-margin categories

## 7. CORRELATION ANALYSIS

### Key Relationships

Our statistical analysis reveals important relationships between pricing, discounts, and sales performance:

**Significant Correlations:**

- **Total Amount ↔ Sale Price:** 0.662 (Strong positive)
  - Higher-priced products drive greater total revenue

- **Total Amount ↔ Quantity:** 0.588 (Moderate positive)
  - Quantity is a key revenue driver, especially for discounted products

- **Discount % ↔ Quantity:** 0.389 (Moderate positive)
  - Discounts effectively drive volume sales

- **Discount % ↔ Sale Price:** -0.160 (Weak negative)
  - Lower-priced items receive higher discount percentages

### Strategic Implications

1. Focus on premium product expansion to maximize revenue per transaction
2. Use strategic discounting to drive volume in mid-tier price ranges
3. Maintain pricing discipline on high-value items while offering targeted promotions
